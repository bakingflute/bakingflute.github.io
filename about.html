About Me!

A Little Bit About Me :)

my process & philosophy

i believe in a human-centered approach to development. this means starting every project by deeply understanding the user's needs and pain points, iterating rapidly, and prioritizing accessibility and performance from day one.


my expertise lies in bridging the gap between data and human stories. continuous learning, clean architecture, and rigorous testing are non-negotiables in my workflow.

life & hobbies

Born in Killeen, Texas and raised all over the American South as a military brat, I love traveling and have recently picked up hiking! You can usually catch me with have soft spots for used books (the best ones come with annotations & bookmarks), black coffee, and all things green! outside of class, you can usually find me experimenting with tofu, trying out new boba places (i have a rubric), and being dragged on hikes by my friends.

speaking of creative outlets -- in my free time, i love to paint and draw. i'm always looking for creative outlets that balance the logical structure of software development and research, such as making pointillist portraits or designing digital art.


my core philosophy centers on creating technology that is both delightful and ethically conscious.
